Welcome to CritterCounter

Globally, animal species are disappearing at an alarming rate as a result of human activity. As climate variation alters weather patterns, the seasonal ranges of even the healthiest populations are changing rapidly and are currently poorly tracked. The advent of remote camera traps is expanding the ability of researchers to collect data on animal species in many environments, but organizing and processing this volume of data presents its own challenges.

CritterCounter addresses these challenges by providing an automated image classification tool for processing wildlife camera trap data. Based on a large corpus of labeled camera trap images, our machine vision model identifies the species in every shot. Designed in consultation with wildlife researchers in Washington State, CritterCounter allows users to spend less time cleaning and organizing their data and more time learning from it.
